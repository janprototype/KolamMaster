üöÄ Getting Started

What is Kolam Master?
Kolam Master is an AI-powered mobile app that teaches you how to create traditional South Indian Kolam patterns. It combines step-by-step tutorials with real-time AI feedback to help you master this 5,000-year-old art form faster than traditional learning methods.
Do I need any drawing experience?
No! Kolam Master is designed for complete beginners. Our AI-guided lessons start with simple patterns and gradually increase in complexity as you improve. Whether you've never drawn before or you're an experienced artist, you'll find appropriate challenges.
How do I get started?
Simply sign up for early access on our homepage, download the app when it launches, create your account, and start with the beginner tutorials. The app will guide you through everything step-by-step.

‚ú® Features & Functionality

How does the AI feedback work?
Our AI, powered by Google Gemini, analyzes your drawing by comparing it to reference patterns. It evaluates stroke accuracy, pattern completion, symmetry, and technique, then provides personalized suggestions for improvement. You get instant feedback on what you're doing well and specific areas to focus on.
Can I practice offline?
Yes! You can download patterns for offline practice. Your progress will be saved locally and synced to the cloud when you're back online. Note that AI validation requires an internet connection.
How many Kolam patterns are included?
The app includes over 300 traditional Kolam patterns, ranging from simple beginner designs to complex advanced patterns. New patterns are added regularly based on user requests and cultural research.
Can I create my own designs?
Yes! Once you've mastered the basics, you can use the freehand drawing mode to create your own Kolam designs. You can save them to your personal gallery and share them with the community.

üì± Technical Questions

What devices are supported?
Kolam Master is available for:
  • iOS devices (iPhone and iPad) running iOS 13 or later
  • Android devices running Android 8.0 or later
  • Tablets are especially recommended for the best drawing experience
Do I need a stylus?
No, you can draw with your finger. However, a stylus (like Apple Pencil or any capacitive stylus) provides more precision and a more natural drawing experience, especially for detailed patterns.
How much storage space does the app require?
The base app is approximately 150MB. Additional space is needed for downloaded patterns and saved drawings, typically 200-500MB depending on usage.
Can I use the app on multiple devices?
Yes! Your account syncs across all your devices. You can start practicing on your phone and continue on your tablet, with all your progress automatically saved.

üìö Learning & Progress

How long does it take to learn Kolam?
It varies by individual, but most users achieve basic proficiency in 2-3 weeks with regular practice (15-30 minutes daily). Intermediate skills typically develop in 4-6 weeks, and advanced mastery in 2-3 months. This is significantly faster than traditional learning methods.
How often should I practice?
We recommend practicing 15-30 minutes daily for best results. Consistent short sessions are more effective than occasional long sessions. The app tracks your practice streaks and sends gentle reminders.
What if I get stuck on a pattern?
The app provides multiple support options:
  • Contextual hints that guide without spoiling
  • Slow-motion animation playback
  • Step-by-step breakdowns
  • AI suggestions for common mistakes
  • Community forums for peer support

üåè Cultural & Educational

Are these authentic traditional patterns?
Yes! All patterns in Kolam Master are based on authentic traditional designs, researched and verified by cultural experts. We work with Kolam practitioners and cultural organizations to ensure authenticity and respect for the art form's heritage.
Do I need to be South Indian to learn Kolam?
Not at all! Kolam is a beautiful art form that anyone can learn and appreciate. The app provides cultural context and history to help all learners understand and respect the tradition, regardless of their background.
Can I learn about the cultural significance?
Yes! Each pattern includes information about its cultural meaning, traditional uses, regional variations, and historical context. We believe understanding the "why" enriches the learning experience.
